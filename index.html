HTML+CSS & Marks+Channels

The data types are items and possibly attributes as the different sector types in each pie chart. The marks are areas used as items, and the channels are position (both x and y) color, size, and tilt (of the sector).

The data types are links, nodes, and attributes. The marks are points and lines, the marks are used as nodes and links, and the channels are color, position, and arguably tilt.

The data types are links and items (nodes). The marks are points and lines, the marks are used as nodes and links, and the channels are color and position relative to the center.

The data types are items and attributes. The attributes are quantitative per each item. The marks are lines and area, the marks are used as items, and the channels are height and color.
